Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this thrilling action-packed adventure game

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game where your skills and strategy determine how long you can last. Face increasingly challenging waves of monsters while collecting power-ups and upgrading your character.

How to Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Automatically attack nearby enemies
  • Collect experience orbs to level up
  • Choose new abilities when leveling up
  • Survive as long as possible!

Game Features

Endless Adventure

Face waves of unique monsters with increasing difficulty

Character Progression

Level up and unlock powerful abilities and upgrades

Strategic Gameplay

Choose your path and build your character's strengths
